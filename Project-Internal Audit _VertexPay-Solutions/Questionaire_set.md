# Interview Questionnaire | IT Director - Access Management

**Organization:** VertexPay Solutions  
**Audit Area:** Access Management  
**Interviewee:** IT Director  
**Interviewer:** Internal Audit Team  
**Date:** 2026-07-21  

## Questions

### 1. Walk me through the process from the moment a new employee joins VertexPay until they receive access to systems such as Microsoft 365, Azure AD, and AWS.

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Who initiates the access request?
- Who approves the access?
- Is the process automated or manual?
- How is the employee's role considered when assigning access?

### 2. Who is responsible for ensuring that access is removed when an employee leaves the organization?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- How does IT receive notification of termination?
- What is the expected timeframe for access removal?
- How is completion verified?
- What happens if the process is delayed?

### 3. How often are user access reviews supposed to be performed?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Who owns the review?
- Which systems and accounts are included?
- Who performs the review?
- What happens when inappropriate access is identified?

### 4. Can you walk me through the Q1 and Q2 access review cycles?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Was the Q1 review completed?
- Was the Q2 review completed?
- If Q2 was not completed, what prevented it from taking place?
- Was the missed review escalated to management?
- Was any compensating review performed?

### 5. Can you show me evidence of the most recent completed access review and explain how exceptions were handled?

**Evidence to Request:**
- Completed access review
- Reviewer sign-off
- Exception list
- Remediation tickets
- Closure evidence

### 6. How are privileged accounts managed across Azure AD, AWS, and Microsoft 365?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Who can approve privileged access?
- Are administrator accounts separately identified?
- How often are privileged accounts reviewed?
- Are emergency or break-glass accounts included?

### 7. What are the biggest challenges in operating the access management process?

**Potential Areas to Explore:**
- Manual processes
- Lack of ownership
- Incomplete system integration
- HR notification delays
- Competing operational priorities
- Inadequate review tracking

# Interview Questionnaire | Head of Vendor Risk - Vendor Management

**Organization:** VertexPay Solutions  
**Audit Area:** Vendor Management  
**Interviewee:** Head of Vendor Risk  
**Interviewer:** Internal Audit Team  
**Date:** 2026-07-21  

## Questions

### 1. Walk me through the vendor onboarding process from the moment a new vendor is proposed until the vendor is approved.

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Who performs the initial risk assessment?
- How is the vendor risk classification determined?
- When does security due diligence occur?
- Who approves high-risk vendors?

### 2. How does VertexPay determine whether a vendor is low, medium, high, or critical risk?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Is access to customer PII considered?
- Is payment data considered?
- Is system connectivity considered?
- Is business criticality considered?

### 3. How often are vendors reassessed after the initial onboarding assessment?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Is the frequency based on vendor risk?
- Are critical vendors reassessed more frequently?
- Who tracks reassessment due dates?
- What happens when a reassessment is overdue?

### 4. Can you show me evidence of the most recent reassessment for a high-risk or critical vendor?

**Evidence to Request:**
- Reassessment report
- Updated security questionnaire
- SOC report or certification
- Risk rating
- Approval or review record

### 5. How does VertexPay monitor vendor risk between formal assessments?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Are security incidents monitored?
- Are changes in vendor ownership or services tracked?
- Are security certifications monitored for expiry?
- Are vendor breaches or regulatory events reviewed?

### 6. How is the third-party payment processor monitored given its importance to the business and payment environment?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- Is its PCI DSS compliance status monitored?
- Are independent assurance reports reviewed?
- Is the vendor subject to enhanced oversight?
- How are material changes communicated to VertexPay?

### 7. What are the biggest challenges in the current vendor risk management process?

**Potential Areas to Explore:**
- Large vendor population
- Manual tracking
- Lack of reassessment resources
- Unclear ownership
- Procurement and security coordination
- Lack of automated reminders
