# Risk Matrix Definitions : NorthPeak Outfitters

## Likelihood Scale

| Level | Definition |
|-------|------------|
| Rare | May occur only in exceptional circumstances (once per 5+ years). |
| Unlikely | Could occur occasionally (once every 2–5 years). |
| Possible | Might occur under normal business conditions (once every 1–2 years). |
| Likely | Expected to occur multiple times per year, especially during high-traffic events such as Black Friday. |
| Almost Certain | Expected to occur frequently or continuously without effective controls. |

---

## Impact Scale

| Level | Financial | Operational | Reputational |
|-------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Negligible | Less than **$1,000** loss | No noticeable disruption | No public awareness |
| Minor | **$1,000 – $10,000** loss | Minor service disruption with quick recovery | Internal awareness only |
| Moderate | **$10,000 – $100,000** loss | Partial service outage lasting several hours | Limited customer complaints or local attention |
| Major | **$100,000 – $1,000,000** loss | Significant disruption lasting days and affecting sales | Regional or industry-wide negative attention |
| Severe | More than **$1,000,000** loss | Extended outage, major data breach, or business interruption | National media coverage, customer loss, and regulatory action |

---

## Matrix (Likelihood × Impact = Risk Rating)

| Likelihood \ Impact | Negligible | Minor | Moderate | Major | Severe |
|---------------------|------------|-------|----------|-------|--------|
| **Rare** | Low | Low | Low | Medium | Medium |
| **Unlikely** | Low | Low | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Possible** | Low | Medium | Medium | High | High |
| **Likely** | Medium | Medium | High | High | Critical |
| **Almost Certain** | Medium | High | High | Critical | Critical |

### Risk Rating Legend

-  **Low** – Acceptable risk; monitor periodically.
-  **Medium** – Requires planned mitigation and regular monitoring.
-  **High** – Immediate management attention and mitigation required.
-  **Critical** – Urgent action required; business operations may be significantly impacted if left untreated.
